About Me

Mary Labrada is an Instructional Designer at Teaching and Learning with Technology (TLT). She earned her B.S. in Exercise Science from Rutgers University and her M.Ed. in Instructional Design from the University of Massachusetts Boston Online. As an Instruction Designer Mary advises, trains, and supports Rutgesr facutly with their online, hybrid, and face to face courses.

In her free time Mary is an avid conert-goer. She has seen over 100 concerts in her life time and has averaged more than 25 concerts in a single year. She love music of all genres and has seen everyone from Beyonce and Jay-Z to Metallica, Willie Nelson, and Florence and the Machine. The list goes on and on. To date her favorite concert was Muse performing at the Coachella Music Festival in 2014.
